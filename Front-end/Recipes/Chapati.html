Chapati

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Optional: 1 tsp sugar for softer chapatis

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, salt (and sugar if using).
  2. Gradually add warm water and knead into a soft dough.
  3. Add 1 tbsp oil and continue kneading until smooth. Cover and rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into balls, roll each out into a flat circle.
  5. Heat a pan, place one rolled dough, cook until bubbles form then flip. Brush lightly with oil.
  6. Repeat for all, stacking chapatis in a clean cloth to stay soft.
  7. Serve with stew, beans, or tea.
