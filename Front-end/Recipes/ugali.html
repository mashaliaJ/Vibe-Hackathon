Ugali & Sukuma Wiki

Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 bunch sukuma wiki (collard greens)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot, slowly add maize flour while stirring.
  2. Keep stirring until thick and firm. Let it steam.
  3. For sukuma, fry onions in oil, add sukuma, and salt. Cook 5â€“7 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with ugali.
