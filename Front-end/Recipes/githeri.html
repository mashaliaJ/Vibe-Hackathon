Githeri

Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiled maize (whole kernels)
  • 2 cups boiled beans (any type)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • 1 carrot (diced)
  • 2 potatoes (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tsp curry powder or pilau masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot and fry onions until golden.
  2. Add garlic, tomatoes, and curry powder. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  3. Add carrots and potatoes, cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in boiled maize and beans. Add a little water if needed.
  5. Simmer on low heat until vegetables are tender and flavors combine.
  6. Season with salt and garnish with fresh coriander.
  7. Serve hot as a full meal or with avocado on the side.
