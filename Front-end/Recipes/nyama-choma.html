Nyama Choma

Ingredients

  • 1 kg goat or beef (cut into chunks)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 lemon (juiced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • Salt to taste
  • Chili or pilipili (optional)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the meat with lemon juice, garlic, ginger, oil, salt, and chili (if using). Let it sit for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat a grill (charcoal or gas) until hot.
  3. Grill the meat slowly over medium heat, turning occasionally until well browned and tender.
  4. Serve hot with kachumbari (fresh tomato & onion salad) and ugali.
