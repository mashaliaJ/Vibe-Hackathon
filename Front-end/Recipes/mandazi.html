Mandazi

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • Â½ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp ground cardamom (optional)
  • 1 cup coconut milk (or regular milk)
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 2 tbsp margarine or butter
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and cardamom.
  2. Add margarine/butter and rub into the flour mixture.
  3. Pour in coconut milk (and egg if using) and knead into a soft dough.
  4. Cover dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Roll out dough on a floured surface and cut into triangles or circles.
  6. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry mandazi until golden brown.
  7. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  8. Serve warm with tea or enjoy as a snack anytime.
