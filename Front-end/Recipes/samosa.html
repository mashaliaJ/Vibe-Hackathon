Samosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ¬Ω cup water (for dough)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • ¬Ω tsp salt
  • 250g minced beef or vegetables
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 green chili (optional)
  • Cooking oil (for deep frying)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and water to form a firm dough. Add oil and knead until smooth. Rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, fry onion, garlic, and ginger in oil until golden.
  3. Add minced beef/veggies, curry powder, chili, and salt. Cook until done. Let filling cool.
  4. Roll out the dough and cut into circles or strips. Form cone shapes and fill with the mixture.
  5. Seal the edges with a bit of flour paste (flour + water).
  6. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry samosas until golden brown.
  7. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with chutney or kachumbari.
