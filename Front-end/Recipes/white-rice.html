White Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice (long-grain or basmati)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil or butter (optional)
  • 1 tsp salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a pot, bring water to a boil. Add salt and oil/butter if using.
  3. Add the rice, stir once, then cover with a lid.
  4. Reduce heat to low and let it simmer for 15â€“20 minutes (donâ€™t stir).
  5. Once water is absorbed and rice is soft, turn off the heat.
  6. Fluff rice with a fork and serve hot with stew, beans, or veggies.
